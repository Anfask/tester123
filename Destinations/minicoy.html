Minicoy Island

Minicoy is located approximately 400 km off the coast of Kochi. It is one of the 36 small islets which form part of Lakshadweep group of islands. Minicoy is famous for coral reefs and white sand beaches amidst vast expanse of Arabian sea.

Minicoy is one of the few inhabited islands in Lakshadweep and it is the second largest there. It is best known for tranquillity due to its location as it is far away from mainland. Therefore, A visitor here feels that he is very close to nature and far from hustle and bustle of daily life. It is an unforgettable experience to spend time on virgin and unspoilt beaches surrounded by palm and coconut trees which add to its beauty.

There is an old light out built in 1885 by British is a landmark and also offers great view of Jama masjid, built in medieval era is another great attraction for all visitors to this island.

You can also enjoy water sports facilities here among which, kayaking on traditional boats is a great way of exploring coral reef. Besides kayaking, you can also enjoy scuba diving and snorkelling.

Minicoy Island Facts

Location : It is located 398 km from Kochi.

Latitude : 8'-17' North

Longitude : 73'-04' East

Climate : Tropical 22 - 30°C.

Languages : Mahl

How to get there : Travel to this Island is only possible with fix departure packages which are Samudaram Package and Swaying Palm Package and the package starts from Kochi.

Food & Cuisine : The cuisine is mainly Keralite. Tuna fish is mostly available here so you can have it grilled, baked or fried. Spiced coconut-rich Vegetarian & Non vegetarian food. Curried, fried and barbecued fish.

Tourist Season : The best time to visit minicoy is between the months of October to May because of the favorable climate with less heat.

Things not to do : Consumption of alcoholic beverages is prohibited in all islands. Picking up Corals is a punishable crime.
